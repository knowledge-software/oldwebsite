Most important Reflection/Refraction topics for the AP Physics B exam:

1. The law of reflection:
.....angle of incidence = angle of reflection
.....(these angles are measured with respect to the "normal")
2. A ray is a line that represents the path of light
3. Image formation and ray diagrams for plane and curved mirrors
4. Refraction
5. Index of refraction and Snell's Law
6. Critical angle and total internal reflection
6. Image formation and ray diagrams for lenses
7. In general, for mirrors and lenses:
... Converging => real or virtual images
......... o > f: inverted real images
......... o < f: upright virtual images
... Diverging => virtual images
8. The mirror/lens equation
... 1/o + 1/i = 1/f
9. Magnification
... M = -i/o = hi/ho

Most important Magnetic Induction topics for the AP Physics B exam:

1. A changing magnetic field near a coil induces a voltage in the coil (Farady's Law).
.....EMF~number of turns
.....EMF~speed
2. A voltage will be established (and current will flow) in a coil that opposes the change in flux (Lenz's Law).
3. What is magnetic flux? (Calculate magnetic flux)
....Magnetic flux = BAsin(theta)
4. Apply Faraday's Law
....EMF = -N(change in flux/time)
5. Motional EMF
....EMF = BLv
....which end of a rod moving in an external magnetic field has higher voltage? (RHR: F=qvB)
....calculate emf and force for a rod moving in an external magnetic field has higher voltage (RHR: F=ILBsin(theta))
6. Transformers
....(P)in = (P)out
....(IV)in = (IV)out
....(N2/N1) = (V2/V1) = (I1/I2)
7. Why is power sent at high voltage in transmission lines?
8. How does a generator work? (compare and contrast to a motor)
