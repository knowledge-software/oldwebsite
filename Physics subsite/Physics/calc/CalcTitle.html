Ti 89 Hints and Tricks

As with any tool remember that the calculator is not a replacement for skill. It is the poor craftsperson that blames their equipment for bad performance. The ti89 can help keep track of details in calculation but it isn't a replacement for thinking. These pages illustrate how you can make effective use of the ti89 as a tool to help solve problems. Many of the problems illustrated here may be solved in a variety of ways using the ti89; any particular path shown here is intended to highlight a particular technique. I don't pretend to provide an exhaustive list of ways to use this versitile tool.
