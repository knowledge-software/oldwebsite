Fluid mechanics, on one level, is nothing more than an application the kind of things you have seen already in particle mechanics. The only difference here is that the "object" is a considered as a continuous sheet of material that flows. The prinicpal concepts presented here carry over into thermodynamics and to a degree, electric and magnetic fields.
